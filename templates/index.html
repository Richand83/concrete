The Red Floor was a restoration job that I did many years ago. The owners were unable to find anyone qualified to do the maintenance on it, so they just didn’t do it. Several years passed and they were holding a major event. They started looking around at spiffing up their property. They contacted me, explained that they were having an event at the end of the month and needed to have their floors maintained. When I got there to look at their project, it needed to be refurbished and rejuvenated. It took two days to refurbish and rejuvenate that floor, and we had a very constructive conversation about the maintenance needs. As that property is so far out of town that they can’t get anybody qualified, I have agreed to provide their maintenance service for them.
